# TransitOps: Smart Transport Operations Platform

**Hackathon Duration:** 8 Hours

## Objective
To build a comprehensive, end-to-end transport operations platform that digitizes vehicle, driver, dispatch, maintenance, and expense management. The system strictly enforces business rules and provides real-time operational insights for a modernized fleet workflow.

## User Roles & Responsibilities

- **Fleet Manager:** Oversees fleet assets, schedules maintenance, tracks the vehicle lifecycle, and optimizes overall operational efficiency.
- **Dispatcher / Driver:** Creates and manages trips, securely assigns available vehicles and drivers, and actively monitors deliveries in real-time.
- **Safety Officer:** Ensures driver compliance, tracks license validity and expirations, and actively monitors safety scores across the organization.
- **Financial Analyst:** Reviews detailed operational expenses, tracks fuel consumption, evaluates maintenance costs, and reports on overall profitability.
